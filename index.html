01 — Profile

About

The short version: I make sure ambitious, international teams know what they're building and why — without losing the people doing the building.

I started as a 3D artist, which turned out to be the best possible training for project management I didn't know I was getting — I understand what's actually hard about the work I'm now scheduling, reviewing, and unblocking.

Over five years I moved through project assistant and coordinator roles into full ownership of cross-functional digital delivery, currently at Massive Entertainment, a Ubisoft studio in Malmö. I manage international partner studios, run process and tooling for teams that span disciplines and time zones, and I'm PSM I certified.

I'm now looking to bring that delivery discipline outside of games — into agencies, tech, and product teams building things people actually use.
