01 · Profile

About

The short version: I make sure ambitious, international teams know what they're building and why, without losing the people doing the building.

I started out as a 3D artist. That background still shapes how I manage now, I know what's actually hard about the work I'm scheduling and unblocking, not just how it looks on a plan.

Over five years I moved from project assistant to coordinator to full ownership of cross-functional delivery, currently at Massive Entertainment, a Ubisoft studio in Malmö. I work with international partner studios, keep process and tooling running for teams that span disciplines and time zones, and I'm PSM I certified.

What matters most to me is keeping teams aligned without losing the people actually doing the work.
